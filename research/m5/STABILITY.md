# Stability

## Proved (weak)

Theorem 8.1: if a binary-root depth-two policy has value $V$ and static optimum $N\le V/2+\varepsilon$, then

$$
|v_0-v_1|\le 2\varepsilon,
$$

and each continuation creates at most $2\varepsilon$ extra value in the opposite root branch.

Interpretation: near factor-two saturation forces nearly balanced branch contributions and nearly branch-local continuations.

## Open (metric)

Define an invariant distance from a near-extremal experiment to the equality set (quotiented by natural symmetries) and prove

$$
D_2^{\mathrm{ad}}\ge 1-\varepsilon,\qquad
D_2^{\mathrm{na}}\le\frac1K+\varepsilon
$$

implies distance $\le f_K(\varepsilon)$, or construct a family disproving every proposed $f_K(\varepsilon)\to 0$.

No such $f_K$ is claimed here.
